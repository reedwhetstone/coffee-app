## Thesis

Enterprise AI adoption fails when memory is treated as static storage instead of operational context. A useful second brain compresses judgment, authority, examples, and escalation paths into the surfaces agents and humans actually use while doing work.

## Core beats

1. Knowledge bases preserve information; second brains preserve working context.
2. New hires need judgment transfer, not just document search.
3. Agentic systems amplify the same problem because they only act on retrievable, ranked, authoritative context.
4. The operational pattern is docs-as-code, ADRs, strategy notes, memory, and review loops feeding directly into execution.

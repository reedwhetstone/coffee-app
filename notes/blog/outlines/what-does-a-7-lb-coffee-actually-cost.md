## Thesis

Arabica futures have crashed 31% from their February 2025 peak. The obvious move is to stock up on cheap green coffee. But across 1,071 live catalog listings from 33 suppliers, price correlates almost zero with decision-readiness (r = -0.03). The safest buys in a volatile market aren't the cheapest lots. They're the lots where you actually know what you're getting. Price is the worst metric for sourcing safety.

## Evidence Stack

### Internal data (Purveyors live catalog, 2026-05-08)

- 1,071 rows across 33 suppliers, 45 countries
- Price-to-readiness correlation: r = -0.03 (essentially zero)
- Decision-readiness composite: arrival_date + cupping score + structured processing + purveyor_score ≥ 75
- $8-$12 band: 50% have 2+ readiness signals (highest of any band)
- $20+ band: only 32% have 2+ readiness signals
- Jamaica (median $79/lb): only 13% have 2+ readiness signals
- Colombia (median $11/lb): 57% have 2+ readiness signals
- Best-value listings: under $10 with 3+ readiness signals (arrival, cupping score, structured processing)
- Worst-value listings: $181 Taiwan lot with zero readiness signals

### External sources

1. Trading Economics (May 2026): Arabica at $2.73/lb, down 31% YoY from $4.41 peak Feb 2025. Brazil 2026/27 forecast +12% to 71.4M bags.
2. Akerlof, G.A. (1970). "The Market for 'Lemons': Quality Uncertainty and the Market Mechanism." QJE 84(3), 488-500.
3. Perfect Daily Grind (2018). "Green Coffee Pricing Transparency Is Critical (And Complicated)." Jon Allen (Onyx): "No one really shares anything about buying green coffee... many people have really no idea what something is worth."
4. SCA/Emory Transaction Guide (coffeebuyers.org): farmer-donated cost-of-production data, only covers price, not product metadata.

### Cross-industry analogy

Akerlof's lemons: when buyers can't observe quality pre-purchase and sellers control disclosure, markets select against quality. Green coffee has a variant: low-information listings drive out high-information comparisons. The buyer defaults to price and vibes.
